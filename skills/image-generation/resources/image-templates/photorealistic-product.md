## Photography Best Practices

### Camera & Lens Guidelines
- **Product close-ups**: 85-100mm macro lens for detail
- **Lifestyle context**: 50mm for natural perspective
- **Full product**: 35-50mm for minimal distortion

### Lighting Techniques
- **Three-point setup**: Key, fill, and rim light for dimension
- **Soft boxes**: Diffused light for even coverage
- **Reflectors**: Fill shadows, add catchlights
- **Rim light**: Separates product from background

### Material-Specific Tips
| Material | Lighting Approach | Notes |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| Metal | Soft, gradient lighting | Avoid harsh reflections |
| Glass | Backlight or side light | Show transparency |
| Fabric | Soft, raking light | Emphasize texture |
| Wood | Warm, directional light | Show grain |
| Leather | Soft with highlight | Show texture and quality |

### Color Grading
- **Clean/modern**: Neutral, slightly cool
- **Luxury**: Warm shadows, cool highlights
- **Natural**: Warm, slightly desaturated
- **Bold**: High contrast, vibrant

**Recommended Model**: Pro for commercial-quality output
**Person Generation**: `dont_allow` (product focus)
**File Format**: PNG
**Color Space**: sRGB

**Recommended Resolutions:**
- Hero/advertising: 4K (4096×4096 or equivalent)
- E-commerce: 1200×1200px minimum
- Catalog: 2000×2000px

**Aspect Ratios by Use Case:**
- Product listing: 1:1 (square)
- Hero banner: 16:9 or 3:1
- Instagram: 4:5 (portrait)
- Website: 16:9 (landscape)
