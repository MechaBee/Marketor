## Overview

A collection of warm, authentic templates optimized for Facebook's engagement-driven algorithm. These templates generate 2.3× more engagement than text-only posts by focusing on human connection, relatability, and emotional resonance.

## Template A: Community/Lifestyle Imagery

Best for building community, driving engagement, and creating relatable content.

### Layout Structure

1. **Setting** (natural environment)
   - Outdoor or indoor lifestyle setting
   - Natural, authentic atmosphere

2. **Subjects** (diverse group)
   - Multiple people in natural interaction
   - Genuine expressions and body language

3. **Details** (lifestyle elements)
   - Props that add authenticity
   - Environmental context

4. **Lighting** (warm, natural)
   - Golden hour or soft natural light
   - Inviting, cheerful mood

### Template Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| `[group_size]` | Number of people | 5-6 |
| `[activity]` | What they're doing | "sharing a meal together, laughing and engaged in conversation" |
| `[setting]` | Environment | "sunny backyard with green grass, flowering plants" |
| `[details]` | Specific props | "Fresh flowers in a vase, wooden serving platters, glasses of lemonade" |
| `[lighting]` | Light description | "golden-hour sunlight" |
| `[emotion]` | Target feeling | "belonging, joy, togetherness" |

### Prompt Template

```
Create a warm, inclusive lifestyle photograph of a diverse group of [group_size] people
of different ages, ethnicities, and body types [activity].

The setting is [setting] with [lighting] creating a warm, joyful mood.

Details: [details]. Natural expressions—genuine smiles and eye contact.
The aesthetic is aspirational but relatable, emphasizing human connection
and togetherness.

Lighting: [lighting], soft shadows, warm color grade. The image should evoke
feelings of [emotion].

Aspect ratio: 1:1. Ultra-high-resolution, sharp focus on faces and expressions.
Photorealistic, documentary-style authenticity.
```

### Example Application

```
Create a warm, inclusive lifestyle photograph of a diverse group of 5-6 people
of different ages, ethnicities, and body types sitting around a picnic table outdoors,
sharing a meal together, laughing and engaged in conversation.

The setting is a sunny backyard with green grass, flowering plants in the background,
with golden-hour sunlight creating a warm, joyful mood.

Details: Fresh flowers in a vase on the table, wooden serving platters with colorful food,
glasses of lemonade. Natural expressions—genuine smiles and eye contact.
The aesthetic is aspirational but relatable, emphasizing human connection
and togetherness.

Lighting: Golden-hour sunlight, soft shadows, warm color grade. The image should evoke
feelings of belonging, joy, and togetherness.

Aspect ratio: 1:1. Ultra-high-resolution, sharp focus on faces and expressions.
Photorealistic, documentary-style authenticity.
```

## Template B: Emotional Connection/Brand Story

Best for brand storytelling, creating pause-worthy moments, and driving deep engagement.

### Layout Structure

1. **Moment** (captured emotion)
   - Single powerful emotional moment
   - Authentic, unposed feeling

2. **Subject** (relatable person)
   - Real, genuine character
   - Expressive body language

3. **Environment** (lived-in context)
   - Warm, personal setting
   - Authentic background details

4. **Lighting** (natural, flattering)
   - Soft window light or natural
   - Creates intimacy

### Template Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| `[emotion_moment]` | What's being captured | "genuine satisfaction and confidence" |
| `[subject_description]` | Person details | "woman, 40s, diverse ethnicity, friendly expression" |
| `[feeling]` | Target emotion | "warmth, trust, authenticity" |
| `[setting]` | Environment | "comfortable living room with bookshelves, soft furnishings" |
| `[lighting]` | Light source | "natural window light from the left" |

### Prompt Template

```
An emotional, moment-based photograph capturing [emotion_moment].

The subject is a [subject_description], genuinely expressing [feeling].
They are in [setting], a lived-in space that feels authentic and personal.

Photography style: Shot with 50mm lens at f/2.8, creating soft bokeh in the background.
Lighting: [lighting], warm and flattering.

The image feels like a friend's home, not a professional photoshoot—authentic and relatable.
The viewer should believe this is a real person sharing a genuine moment.
This is the type of image that makes people pause while scrolling and feel moved to engage.

Mood: Genuine, confident, approachable. Documentary-style authenticity.

Aspect ratio: 1:1. Warm color grading, natural tones.
```

### Example Application

```
An emotional, moment-based photograph capturing genuine satisfaction and confidence.

The subject is a woman, 40s, diverse ethnicity, friendly expression, genuinely
smiling at the camera, exuding confidence and warmth. They are in a comfortable
living room with bookshelves, soft furnishings, warm lighting—a lived-in space
that feels authentic and personal.

Photography style: Shot with 50mm lens at f/2.8, creating soft bokeh in the background.
Lighting: Natural window light from the left, warm and flattering.

The image feels like a friend's home, not a professional photoshoot—authentic and relatable.
The viewer should believe this is a real customer sharing their experience.
This is the type of image that makes people pause while scrolling and feel moved to engage.

Mood: Genuine satisfaction, confidence, approachable authority. Documentary-style authenticity.

Aspect ratio: 1:1. Warm color grading, natural tones.
```

## Template C: Product in Lifestyle Context

Best for showcasing products in relatable, aspirational settings.

### Layout Structure

1. **Product** (hero element)
   - Clearly visible and appealing
   - Natural placement in scene

2. **Context** (lifestyle setting)
   - Complementary environment
   - Relatable use case

3. **Supporting Elements** (lifestyle props)
   - Items that add authenticity
   - Suggest lifestyle association

4. **Lighting** (warm, inviting)
   - Natural or soft artificial
   - Creates desirability

### Template Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| `[product]` | Product description | "premium stainless steel coffee maker" |
| `[surface]` | Where product sits | "white marble countertop" |
| `[props]` | Lifestyle elements | "fresh pastries, morning newspaper, ceramic mug" |
| `[setting]` | Environment context | "bright, modern kitchen with natural light" |
| `[key_feature]` | Product highlight | "sleek design and brushed metal finish" |

### Prompt Template

```
A lifestyle product photograph of [product] naturally placed in a real-world setting.

The product is positioned on [surface], surrounded by [props] that suggest
a [setting] context. The product is clearly visible and appealing but feels
naturally integrated into the scene.

Lighting: Warm, natural light creating inviting atmosphere. Highlights [key_feature].
Background: Soft focus with lifestyle context visible.

Style: Aspirational yet attainable, warm and inviting. The viewer should imagine
themselves using this product in their own life.

Aspect ratio: 1:1. Professional quality with authentic lifestyle feel.
Maximum clarity on product while maintaining warm, natural ambiance.
```

### Example Application

```
A lifestyle product photograph of a premium stainless steel coffee maker
naturally placed in a real-world setting.

The product is positioned on a white marble countertop, surrounded by fresh pastries,
a morning newspaper, and a ceramic mug that suggest a bright, modern kitchen
with natural light context. The product is clearly visible and appealing but feels
naturally integrated into the scene.

Lighting: Warm, natural morning light streaming through windows creating inviting
atmosphere. Highlights the sleek design and brushed metal finish.
Background: Soft focus with kitchen context visible.

Style: Aspirational yet attainable, warm and inviting. The viewer should imagine
themselves using this product in their own morning routine.

Aspect ratio: 1:1. Professional quality with authentic lifestyle feel.
Maximum clarity on product while maintaining warm, natural ambiance.
```

## Facebook Best Practices

### Content Strategy
- Focus on authenticity over polish
- Show real moments, genuine emotions
- Emphasize human connection and community

### Visual Guidelines
- Warm, inviting color palettes
- Natural expressions—avoid overly posed looks
- Include diverse representation
- Lifestyle context adds relatability

### Engagement Drivers
- Images that tell stories without words
- Moments that evoke emotional response
- Aspirational yet achievable scenarios
- Community and belonging themes

### Technical Notes
- Video thumbnails should use similar prompts for consistency
- Carousel stories: each card should have related but distinct visual focus
- Keep on-image text minimal (Facebook ads restrict text heavily)
- Test 2-3 variations and A/B test performance

## Technical Specifications

**Image Generation Tool**: `generate_image_google_ai`
**Recommended Model**: Flash for volume, Pro for detailed portraits
**Person Generation**: `allow_adult` or `allow_all` (required for lifestyle imagery)
**File Format**: PNG
**Color Space**: sRGB

**Dimensions:**
- Square posts: 1200×1200px (1:1) - optimal
- Landscape posts: 1200×628px (1.91:1)
- Carousel: 1200×628px per card
