# Service Page Template

Use this template for professional services, consulting, support packages, or similar service-based offerings.

## Template Guidance

**Purpose**: Generate service inquiry or consultation booking
**Primary CTA**: "Get a Quote", "Schedule Consultation", "Contact Us", "Book a Call"
**Focus**: Expertise, process, results, trust-building

## Section Definitions

### Hero Section
**Goal**: Communicate service value and establish credibility

**Elements**:
- Headline: Service name + outcome delivered
- Subheadline: Target audience + problem solved
- Primary CTA: Consultation/contact focused
- Hero image: Team/expert in action or client success

**Example Structure**:
```
# [Service Name]: [Outcome Delivered]

[Subheadline: Who you help and how they benefit]

[Primary CTA: "Schedule Free Consultation" | "Get Your Quote" | "Contact Us"]

[hero-image: Professional team, client success, or service delivery]
```

### Benefits Section
**Goal**: Show service value and business impact

**Elements**:
- 3-5 key service benefits
- Business outcomes focus (ROI, efficiency, growth)
- Industry-specific pain points addressed
- Differentiation from competitors

**Framework**:
- Benefit 1: Strategic outcome (growth, savings)
- Benefit 2: Process improvement
- Benefit 3: Risk mitigation/expertise access

### Features Section
**Goal**: Detail service scope and deliverables

**Elements**:
- 4-6 service components/deliverables
- What's included in service
- Process or methodology overview
- Timeline/duration if applicable

**Format**: Service component → What you get → Why it matters

### Social Proof Section
**Goal**: Demonstrate expertise and track record

**Elements**:
- Client case studies or results
- Industry recognition/certifications
- Years of experience/projects completed
- Client testimonials with specific results
- Company/brand logos served

**Guidelines**:
- Quantify results (X% improvement, $Y saved)
- Show industry diversity or specialization
- Include decision-maker testimonials

### How It Works Section
**Goal**: Clarify service process and reduce uncertainty

**Elements**:
- 3-4 step engagement process
- What client provides/does
- What service team delivers
- Timeline expectations

**Format**:
1. [Initial Step]: Discovery/Assessment
2. [Execution Step]: Implementation/Delivery
3. [Results Step]: Launch/Handoff/Support
4. [Optional]: Ongoing optimization

### CTA Section
**Goal**: Convert to consultation or inquiry

**Elements**:
- Value recap specific to service
- Primary CTA (schedule/contact)
- Secondary CTA (download guide, view case study)
- Trust signals (satisfaction guarantee, confidentiality)
- Next steps clarity

**Framework**:
- Recap transformation delivered
- Address commitment concerns
- Clear contact/booking process
- Respond within X hours commitment

## Content Guidelines

**Tone**: Professional, authoritative, trustworthy
**Length**: More detailed than product pages (expertise demonstration)
**Keywords**: Service-specific terms, industry language
**CTAs**: Consultation-focused, low-commitment entry points

## SEO Considerations

- Include service + location keywords if local
- Industry terminology naturally integrated
- Expertise signals (certifications, experience)
- Internal links to related services
- FAQ section for long-tail keywords

## Trust-Building Elements

**Essential for services**:
- Team credentials/bios
- Process transparency
- Clear pricing or pricing framework
- Satisfaction guarantees
- Privacy/confidentiality assurances
- Industry memberships/affiliations

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

- Vague service descriptions
- No clear process outlined
- Weak or missing expertise signals
- CTA too high-commitment (buy vs consult)
- Missing response time expectations
- Overpromising without proof
