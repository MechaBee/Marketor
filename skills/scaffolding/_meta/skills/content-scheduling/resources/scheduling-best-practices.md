# Platform-Specific Scheduling Best Practices

Detailed timing guidelines for optimal engagement across platforms.

## Facebook

**Optimal Posting Times:**
- **Weekdays:** 9:00-11:00, 13:00-15:00
- **Weekends:** 12:00-13:00
- **Best days:** Wednesday, Thursday, Friday
- **Avoid:** Before 8:00, after 21:00

**Spacing Rules:**
- Minimum 4 hours between posts
- Recommended: 1-2 posts per day maximum

**Content-Type Timing:**
- **Link posts:** Morning (9:00-11:00) - catches commuters
- **Text posts:** Afternoon (13:00-15:00) - lunch break engagement
- **Video posts:** Evening (18:00-20:00) - relaxation time

**Weekly Strategy:**
- Mon-Tue: Lower engagement, avoid if possible
- Wed-Fri: Peak engagement, prioritize important content
- Weekends: Casual content, entertainment focus

## Instagram

**Optimal Posting Times:**
- **Weekdays:** 11:00-13:00, 19:00-21:00
- **Weekends:** 10:00-12:00
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Sunday evenings

**Spacing Rules:**
- Minimum 6 hours between feed posts
- Recommended: 1 feed post per day
- Stories: Multiple daily (no spacing limit)

**Content-Type Timing:**
- **Reels:** Evening (19:00-21:00) - peak scroll time
- **Image posts:** Lunch (12:00-13:00) - quick consumption
- **Carousels:** Afternoon (14:00-16:00) - time for engagement
- **Stories:** Throughout day - ephemeral nature

**Weekly Strategy:**
- Monday: Motivational, week-start content
- Mid-week: Educational, engaging carousels
- Thursday-Friday: Entertainment, weekend prep
- Weekends: Lifestyle, relaxed content

**Algorithm Considerations:**
- First hour critical for engagement
- Reels get longer shelf life
- Stories boost profile visibility
- Carousels encourage saves/shares

---

## Google Ads

**Optimal Launch Times:**
- **Search ads:** 8:00-10:00 - business hours start
- **Display ads:** 10:00-14:00 - peak browsing hours
- **Discovery ads:** 12:00-15:00 - lunch/afternoon scroll
- **Performance Max:** 9:00 - full day optimization window

**Spacing Rules:**
- Minimum 24 hours between campaign launches
- Recommended: 2-3 days for A/B testing separation

**Launch Day Strategy:**
- **Monday-Tuesday:** Best for B2B
- **Wednesday-Thursday:** Best for B2C
- **Friday:** Avoid unless weekend-relevant
- **Weekends:** Only for weekend-specific offers

**Testing Considerations:**
- Allow 3-5 days for initial data
- Launch sequentially, not simultaneously
- Weekday launches preferred for monitoring

## LinkedIn

**Optimal Posting Times:**
- **Weekdays:** 8:00-10:00, 12:00-13:00, 17:00-18:00
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Avoid:** Weekends, Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

**Spacing Rules:**
- Minimum 8 hours between posts
- Recommended: 1 post per day, 3-4 per week

**Content-Type Timing:**
- **Articles:** Morning (8:00-10:00) - commute reading
- **Posts:** Lunch (12:00-13:00) - break time scrolling
- **Videos:** Evening (17:00-18:00) - end of workday

**Weekly Strategy:**
- Monday: Industry news, thought leadership
- Tuesday-Thursday: Educational, engagement-focused
- Friday: Lighter content, week wrap-ups
- Weekends: Avoid unless exceptional content

**Professional Context:**
- Business hours only
- B2B focus: 8-10am best
- Thought leadership: Tuesday-Wednesday
- Job posts: Monday-Tuesday mornings

## Cross-Platform Strategy

**Daily Cadence:**
- **Morning (8-10am):** LinkedIn, Facebook link posts
- **Lunch (12-1pm):** Instagram images, LinkedIn posts
- **Afternoon (2-4pm):** Facebook text posts, Instagram carousels
- **Evening (7-9pm):** Instagram reels, Facebook videos

**Weekly Distribution:**
- Spread platforms across days
- Avoid multiple platforms same time
- Consider audience overlap

**Spacing Guidelines:**
- **Same platform:** 2-3 days minimum
- **Different platforms:** Can post same day
- **Same content type:** 3-5 days apart
- **Major announcements:** Space 1 week apart

**Timezone Considerations:**
- Default: Europe/Budapest (GMT+1)
- Target audience: Adjust accordingly
- International campaigns: Multiple posts for timezones
- Test and optimize based on analytics
