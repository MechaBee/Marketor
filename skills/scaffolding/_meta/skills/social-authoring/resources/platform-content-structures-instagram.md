# Platform Content Structures

Platform-specific content templates and formatting guidance for Instagram posts.

## Metadata Format

All content files use a markdown header (NOT YAML frontmatter) for metadata:

```markdown

**Platform**: instagram
**Format**: ig-reel
**Topic**: brand-setup-tutorial
**Context**: marketor-showcase
**Revision**: 1
**Target Audience**: marketing-professionals
**Objective**: engagement
**CTA URL**: https://example.com/blog/article
**Hashtags**: #Marketing, #YourBrand, #Tutorial
**Associated Media**: image_brand-setup_rev1.png
**Created**: 2025-12-02
**Status**: draft

---

## Post Content

[Platform-specific content structure follows...]
```

## Instagram Platform

### Instagram Reel

```markdown
## Post Content

## Video Description
[Brief description of content, style, trending elements]

## Text Overlay Timeline

### 0-2 seconds
[Problem hook or POV setup]

### 2-4 seconds
[Transition/solution tease]

### 4-7 seconds
[Clear value proposition or reveal]

## Caption

### Hook
[First line appears in feed, optimize for engagement]

### Body
[Elaborate on video, 2-3 lines max]

### CTA
[Action step, often "Link in bio" or "DM us [keyword]"]

### Hashtags
[8-12 mix of trending, niche, and branded hashtags]
```

**Caption Length Optimization:**
- **Optimal**: 138-150 characters (maximum engagement)
- **Critical**: First 125 characters visible before "...more" (optimize hook)
- **Sweet spot**: 125-300 characters (balances information with visibility)
- **Maximum**: 2,200 characters (engagement drops significantly after 300)
- **Mobile feed**: First 100-125 characters visible
- **Recommendation**: Keep captions under 300 characters for best performance
- **Hook priority**: First 125 characters must capture attention and convey value

**Platform Tips:**
- Vertical 9:16 format only
- Trending audio increases discoverability
- Quick cuts maintain attention (average: 7-15 seconds)
- POV format highly engaging
- First line of caption critical (shows in feed)
- Caption should complement video, not repeat it
- Use line breaks for readability
- Hashtags: 3-5 perform better than 30 (avoid clutter)

---

```markdown
[Additional slides 4-N following same pattern]

---

## Slide N (Final - CTA)
**Visual:** [Description of CTA-focused design]

**Text on Slide:**
[Clear call-to-action with value proposition]
[Contact info or next step]
```

**Caption Length Optimization:**
- **Optimal**: 138-150 characters (maximum engagement)
- **Critical**: First 125 characters visible before "...more"
- **Sweet spot**: 150-300 characters (carousel context benefits from slightly longer)
- **Maximum**: 2,200 characters (but keep under 400 for best engagement)
- **Mobile feed**: First 100-125 characters visible
- **Recommendation**: 150-300 characters ideal for carousel storytelling
- **Hook priority**: First line must entice users to swipe through slides

**Platform Tips:**
- Optimal: 5-10 slides (sweet spot: 7)
- Square format (1:1) works best
- Cover slide must hook scrollers
- Each slide should stand alone (users may jump around)
- Save valuable content for middle slides (higher engagement)
- Final slide: clear CTA with branded element
- Caption should tease slide content, not summarize it
- Use emojis strategically for visual breaks
- Hashtags: 5-8 relevant tags (avoid overloading)

### Instagram Story

```markdown
## Post Content

## Story Frame 1
**Visual:** [Description of image/video background]
**Text Overlay:** [Short, punchy text - 5-7 words max]
**Interactive Element:** [Poll/Quiz/Question/Countdown sticker]

## Story Frame 2
**Visual:** [Description continuing narrative]
**Text Overlay:** [Follow-up or answer reveal]
**Interactive Element:** [Link sticker/Swipe up]

## Story Frame 3 (Optional)
**Visual:** [Description of final frame]
**Text Overlay:** [CTA or final message]
**Interactive Element:** [Link to profile/DM prompt]
```

**Text Overlay Length Optimization:**
- **Optimal**: 5-7 words per frame (maximum readability)
- **Maximum**: 15-20 words (text becomes too small)
- **Critical**: Large, bold text that's readable at a glance
- **Mobile**: Users scroll quickly - brevity is essential
- **Recommendation**: One clear message per frame
- **Multi-frame**: Break longer messages across 2-3 frames

**Platform Tips:**
- 24-hour lifespan (ephemeral content)
- Vertical 9:16 format
- Interactive elements boost reach
- Keep text overlays brief and large
- Use highlights to preserve important stories
- Series of 3-5 frames optimal (don't exhaust attention)
- Text should be scannable in 2-3 seconds
- Use stickers and GIFs for engagement
- Link stickers drive traffic (use strategically)

## Instagram Caption Best Practices Summary

### Character Count Guidelines
- **Highest Engagement**: 138-150 characters
- **Visible in Feed**: First 125 characters (before "...more")
- **Mobile Visibility**: First 100-125 characters
- **Sweet Spot**: 125-300 characters (most formats)
- **Maximum**: 2,200 characters (avoid - engagement drops)

### Hook Optimization
- First line must capture attention immediately
- Use curiosity, questions, or bold statements
- Avoid generic openings ("Hey guys!", "Check this out")
- Lead with value proposition or emotional trigger
- Test different hooks for same content

### Hashtag Strategy
- **Optimal**: 3-5 relevant hashtags
- **Maximum**: 30 (but fewer perform better)
- **Research**: Use hashtags with 10k-500k posts (sweet spot)
- **Mix**: Combine trending, niche, and branded tags
- **Placement**: End of caption or first comment (both work)
- **Avoid**: Banned or spammy hashtags

### Engagement Tactics
- Use line breaks for readability
- Ask questions to encourage comments
- Include clear call-to-action
- Tag relevant accounts (sparingly)
- Use emojis strategically (not excessively)
- Post during peak audience activity times
- Respond to comments quickly (boosts reach)

### Content Quality
- Caption should add value beyond the visual
- Tell stories, share insights, educate
- Match tone to brand voice and audience
- Proofread for typos and clarity
- Test different caption styles and lengths
- Analyze performance and iterate
